Hey there! This is a Simon Game. If you dont know how to play here are the instrutions:

  • You can press any key on the keyboard to start playing.
  • Once the game starts. One of the four colored box will blink and make a sound.
  • You have to click on the same block that blinked to move on the next level.
  • You have to remember the sequence of the blinks that happend and click on the exact same pattern.
  • for example: if on level 1 red blinked, level 2 yellow blinked
    then in level one you have to click on red and on level 2 you have to click on red then yellow
  • Level indicates the number of blocks in the pattern
  • Once you loose you can easily restart the game by clicking any key
  • All the best!
